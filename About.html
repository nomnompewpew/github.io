Fort Harrison is a 3 piece experimental rock band from Boise, Idaho. The music fuses psychedelic desert rock and northwest grunge with hints of jazz, funk, alternative and punk. The dynamic shifts, tempo and key changes set the mood for a captivating listening experience from start to finish.
